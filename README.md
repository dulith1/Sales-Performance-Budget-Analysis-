# 📊 Sales Performance & Budget Analysis (Power BI)

This project presents a comprehensive **Sales Performance & Budget Analysis** built entirely using **Power BI**.  
The report enables stakeholders to transition from legacy static reporting into a dynamic visual analytics solution, tracking internet sales performance, comparing actuals against budget targets, and identifying key customer and product trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to create a Power BI–based reporting solution that:

- Moves internet sales reporting from static spreadsheets to interactive dashboards  
- Tracks total sales, top-performing products, and highest-value customers  
- Compares actual sales performance against a fixed **2021 budget**  
- Analyzes historical sales trends over the previous two years (**2019–2020**)  
- Supports Agile user stories for **Sales Managers** and **Sales Representatives** to track targets and follow up with top customers  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- **Power BI**
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**

- **Data Modeling (Star Schema)**
- **KPI Design & Variance Analysis**
- **Interactive Dashboard Design**

---

## 📊 Dashboard 1: Sales Overview Dashboard

The **Sales Overview Dashboard** provides a high-level executive summary of overall internet sales performance versus budget.

### Key Metrics & Visuals:
- **Sales vs Budget KPI**  
  - Total Sales: **$16.35M**  
  - Budget: **$15.3M**  
  - Positive variance highlighting overperformance
- **Sales vs Budget by Month** (line chart)
- **Sales by Product Category** (donut chart – Bikes, Accessories, Clothing)
- **Top 10 Customers & Products** (bar charts)
- **Sales by Customer City** (global map visualization)

---

## 📈 Dashboard 2: Customer Overview Dashboard

The **Customer Overview Dashboard** is designed for Sales Representatives to analyze individual customer behavior and purchasing patterns.

### Key Features:
- **Customer Heatmap Matrix**  
  - Month-by-month breakdown of customer purchases  
  - Helps identify retention patterns and buying frequency
- **Dynamic Slicers**  
  - Year  
  - Month  
  - Customer City  
  - Product Category  
  - Sub-Category  

---

## 📂 Supporting Data & Model

The project uses a structured **star schema** data model with Fact and Dimension tables to support:

- Historical internet sales analysis (**2019–2021**)  
- Budget vs actual comparisons using a 2021 budget dataset  
- Advanced DAX calculations and time-intelligence measures  
- Drill-downs and dynamic filtering across visuals
